Privacy Policy — Burq Todo

Effective date: 2025-11-04

Overview

Burq Todo stores task data only on the device. This app does not send your tasks or personal data to any server, third party, or analytics provider.

Data collected

No personal data is collected or transmitted by Burq Todo. Tasks and their completion state are saved locally on your device.

How data is stored

Task data is stored locally using the device's local storage (shared preferences). If you uninstall the app, local data may be removed by the system.

Third parties

Burq Todo does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or trackers in the provided code. If third-party services are added later, this policy must be updated.

Permissions

The app does not require network permissions in its current form. Only local storage is used. If you add permissions later (e.g., cloud backup), update this policy and notify users.

Children

This app is not designed specifically for children. If you believe your child provided personal information through the app, contact the developer below.
